Input

The input consists of a single line containing three integers $n$ ($2 \leq n \leq 10\, 000$), the length of the sides of the square cake in centimeters, $h$ ($0 < h < n$), the distance of the horizontal cut from the top edge of the cake in centimeters, and $v$ ($0 < v < n$), the distance of the vertical cut from the left edge of the cake in centimeters. Each cake is $4$ centimeters thick.

Output

Display the volume (in cubic centimeters) of the largest of the four pieces of cake after the horizontal and vertical cuts are made.
